They keep your feet warm, they keep them cushioned, and they sometimes accessorize. New socks are a lot more comfortable to wear, so do remember to replace your old ones every once in a while. If you want something affordable, colorful, and made with decent quality, they go for about $2.50 a pair at [UNIQLO](http://www.uniqlo.com/us/).
